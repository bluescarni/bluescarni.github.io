Detailed Description

template<typename Series, typename T>
struct piranha::math::pow_impl< Series, T, typename std::enable_if< is_series< Series >::value >::type >

Specialisation of the piranha::math::pow() functor for piranha::series.

This specialisation is activated when Series is an instance of piranha::series. The result will be computed via the series' pow() method.
