template<typename Series>
struct piranha::math::integrate_impl< Series, typename std::enable_if< is_instance_of< Series, poisson_series >::value >::type >

Specialisation of the piranha::math::integrate() functor for Poisson series.

This specialisation is activated when Series is an instance of piranha::poisson_series.
