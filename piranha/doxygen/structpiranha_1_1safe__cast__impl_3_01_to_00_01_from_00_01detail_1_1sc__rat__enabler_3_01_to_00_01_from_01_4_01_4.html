Detailed Description

template<typename To, typename From>
struct piranha::safe_cast_impl< To, From, detail::sc_rat_enabler< To, From > >

Specialisation of piranha::safe_cast() for conversions involving piranha::mp_rational.

This specialisation is enabled in the following cases:

  • To is a rational type and From is either an arithmetic type or piranha::mp_integer,
  • To is an integral type or piranha::mp_integer, and From is piranha::mp_rational.
