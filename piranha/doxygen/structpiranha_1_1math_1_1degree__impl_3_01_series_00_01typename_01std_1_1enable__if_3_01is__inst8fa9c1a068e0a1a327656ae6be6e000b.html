Detailed Description

template<typename Series>
struct piranha::math::degree_impl< Series, typename std::enable_if< is_instance_of< Series, power_series >::value >::type >

Specialisation of the piranha::math::degree() functor for instances of piranha::power_series.

This specialisation is activated if Series is an instance of piranha::power_series. If Series does not fulfill the requirements outlined in piranha::power_series, the call operator will be disabled.
